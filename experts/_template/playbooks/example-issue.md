## Diagnosis

This is the template playbook. It exists to demonstrate the shape of a real one
and to give the `_template` expert something concrete to match against.

A real playbook documents a recurring, recognizable issue: the symptoms a caller
would describe, the keywords that identify it, the steps to confirm it, and the
fix.

## Steps to confirm

1. Reproduce the reported symptom.
2. Run the `gather-example` skill to collect supporting data
   (`scripts/collect_example.sh`).
3. Compare the collected values against the expected baseline below.
